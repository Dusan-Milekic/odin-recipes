This burger is so tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion (optional toppings)

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef with salt and pepper.
  3. Form the mixture into 4 equal patties.
  4. Grill the patties for about 5-6 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
  5. If using cheese, add a slice on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Assemble the burgers with your choice of toppings.
