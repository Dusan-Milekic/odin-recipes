This pasta is delightful!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes to the pan, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the tomato sauce. Toss well to combine.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
